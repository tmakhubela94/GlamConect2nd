Frequently Asked Questions

Do all Glamsquad memberships automatically renew?

Yes, all Glamsquad memberships automatically renew on a monthly basis after any prepaid time has ended. Prepaid time may include 1 month, 3 months, or yearly

What is the Glamsquad membership?

The Glamsquad membership is the perfect way for you to get your favorite blowouts for less on a monthly basis. Plus, you can enjoy exclusive members-only perks such as a 10% discount on additional hair, nail and makeup services.

How do I purchase the Glamsquad membership?

Under the Services menu on Glamsquad.com or in the Glamsquad app, click Membership. Add the Membership option to your cart and check out to purchase.

How are clients rated?

Pros rate a client after each appointment, so you've always got a say. We also require clients to provide a valid credit card for identity verification purposes.

Can I use a promo code to purchase Glamsquad products?

At this time, Glamsquad promotional codes do not apply to products. Our promotional codes only applies to services unless specifically stated in the promotional emai

Can my package be refunded?

Glamsquad packages are good for a lifetime! Your package never expires. Partially used packages and unused packages are non-refundable
